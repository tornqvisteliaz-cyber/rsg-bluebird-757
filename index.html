WHO WE ARE

RSG is a small team of passionate flight sim enthusiasts.

We create the highest fidelity add-ons for Microsoft Flight Simulator, X-Plane and Prepar3D. Every project is built with obsessive attention to detail — from accurate flight dynamics and systems simulation to stunning liveries and sound design.

Our flagship project, Bluebird 757, brings the iconic Icelandair Boeing 757-200 to life with unmatched accuracy and visual quality.
